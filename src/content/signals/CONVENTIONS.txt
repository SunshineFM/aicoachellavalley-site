Signals content conventions (v1 scaffold)

Future markdown signals should be stored as one file per signal in this folder.

Suggested filename convention:
- YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug.md

Suggested frontmatter keys:
- title
- timestamp
- city
- sector
- sources
- summary
- confidence

Allowed city values (canonical 9):
- Palm Springs
- Desert Hot Springs
- Cathedral City
- Rancho Mirage
- Palm Desert
- Indian Wells
- Indio
- La Quinta
- Coachella

Recommended frontmatter keys (minimum for Briefs UX):
- city
- sector
- summary

Example frontmatter:
---
title: Regional operator launches AI pilot
date: 2026-02-17
city: Palm Springs
sector: Healthcare
summary: A short, verifiable update describing what changed and why it matters.
signal_type: adoption
confidence: medium
---

Canonical city values + slugs:
- Palm Springs -> palm-springs
- Desert Hot Springs -> desert-hot-springs
- Cathedral City -> cathedral-city
- Rancho Mirage -> rancho-mirage
- Palm Desert -> palm-desert
- Indian Wells -> indian-wells
- Indio -> indio
- La Quinta -> la-quinta
- Coachella -> coachella

Sector guidance:
- Sector is freeform for now. Use a clear human-readable label; normalization is handled later.

Minimal frontmatter example:
---
title: Brief title goes here
date: 2026-02-18
---

Ideal frontmatter example:
---
title: Regional operator launches AI pilot
date: 2026-02-18
summary: One-sentence summary of what changed and why it matters.
city: Palm Springs
sector: Healthcare
sources:
  - https://example.org/source-1
updatedAt: 2026-02-18T15:00:00Z
provenance: reporting
confidence: medium
---
